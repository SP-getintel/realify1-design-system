Foundations

Color

A two-tone palette: deep navy carries authority, a single amber owns every CTA and conversion moment. Neutrals are derived from the navy hue (250°) so surfaces always feel cool, never gray-dead.

Brand

Use navy for primary surfaces and dense UI. Reserve amber for one moment per frame — never two ambers competing.
