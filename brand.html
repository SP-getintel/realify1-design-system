Brand

Logo & iconography

The Realify mark is a lockup — a house silhouette + amber rising arrow + the wordmark in Manrope-bold navy. Never split the icon from the wordmark in product surfaces. For geography (Australia-only), the Realify Australia silhouette is rendered via CSS mask so it inherits currentColor.

Lockup & backgrounds

Primary lockup on white. Inverted (white) lockup on navy or amber via filter: brightness(0) invert(1). Don't recolor the house or arrow individually.

Primary · white
Marketing site, body content, light app surfaces.
Inverted · navy
Footer, hero, sidebar, dark email templates.
Inverted · amber
Rare — reserve for high-impact moments (launch banner, swag).
Clearspace & sizing

Minimum clearspace = the height of the wordmark's "R". In product, the lockup sits at 48px tall in the navbar and 40–47px in the footer. Don't go below 32px tall — the arrow gets muddy.

Dashed line shows the minimum clearspace. Pad equally on all four sides.

Don'ts

Three quick misuses to avoid.

Don't · stretch
Never distort the lockup vertically or horizontally.
Don't · recolor
Don't shift the brand hues. Use the inverted lockup if you need a different background.
Don't · low contrast
Don't place the primary lockup on amber. Use the inverted version instead.
