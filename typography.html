Foundations

Typography

Two families, both Google Fonts, both grotesque sans. Manrope handles every heading and brand moment; Inter handles every word longer than a button. Tight letter-spacing on headings sells the "engineered, not flashy" tone.

Families

Manrope sits a touch tighter than Inter at the same nominal size — pair them in the documented hierarchy and they read like one voice.
