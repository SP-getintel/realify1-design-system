Foundations

Voice & patterns

Realify talks to busy agents. Plain English, outcome-led, never marketing-y. A single big promise per surface, anchored by one amber word.

The four voice pillars
Direct

Verbs, not vibes

"Books appointments." "Replies in 60 seconds." Concrete moves an agent can picture in their calendar.

Outcome-led

Dollars or hours

Frame benefits as listings won, hours saved, appointments booked. Skip the AI-hype talk.

Local

Australian, not American

Suburb names, "agency" not "brokerage", AUD pricing. The Australia silhouette is a reminder, not decoration.

Calm

One promise per surface

Resist stacking three CTAs. One amber moment per frame; everything else supports it.

Say this · not that

SAY

  • Turn your leads into new listings.
  • Realify reactivates your database.
  • Book a 15-minute live demo.
  • Built for Australian agents.
  • 48 qualified appointments this week.

DON'T SAY

  • Unlock the power of next-gen AI.
  • Revolutionary intelligent automation platform.
  • Schedule a synergistic consultation.
  • Enterprise-grade for the global real-estate vertical.
