Foundations

Spacing, radii & elevation

A 4px base unit, a single radius root (0.75rem) that scales up and down, and four shadow levels — most surfaces sit at level 0 or 1. Generous vertical rhythm on marketing sections; tight density inside the agent portal.

Radii

All radii derive from --radius: 0.75rem. Default card is rounded-xl; hero dashboard uses rounded-2xl; pills (badges, ping-glow) are 9999px.
